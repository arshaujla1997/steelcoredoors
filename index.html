Expert Diagnostics

Safety & Preventative Upkeep

Avoid expensive long-term tracking failures with these basic baseline structural checks.

1. Lubricate Moving Tracks

Apply premium silicone spray to rollers, hinge joints, and spring components twice a year to keep movement completely friction-free.

2. Clear Tracking Debris

Ensure the physical side tracks remain clear of dirt or rust blockages, preventing panels from slipping off alignment unexpectedly.

3. Test Balance Regularly

Pull the manual release cord and raise the door halfway. If it fails to hold its position, your tension springs require professional calibration.

Frequently Asked Questions

How fast can a technician arrive for an emergency?
Our mobile emergency teams are stationed across Perth and generally respond to urgent blockages or spring snaps within 45 to 60 minutes.
Should I replace my garage springs myself?
No. Garage torsion assemblies operate under intense structural tension and can cause severe property damage or injury without proper tools. Always leave this to certified technicians.
Do your repairs come with a service warranty?
Yes, absolutely. All our premium grade parts, steel cables, and replacement automatic drive motors feature structured operational warranties.
